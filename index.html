My Morning Routine

  1. I tend to wake up naturally 7.5 hours after I have gone to bed, making my wake up time around 6:00am every morning.
  2. I will then make my bed, open my blinds, and make a double espresso.
  3. 4 days out of the week I will go to the gym for 7:00am. On days where I don't go, I will do some school work while listening to classical music.
  4. By 8:30am I will either be done at the gym, or wrapping up what I am working on and then make breakfast which is usually banana and peanut butter on toast
  5. Lastly, I will take a shower and get dressed for the rest of my day :)
